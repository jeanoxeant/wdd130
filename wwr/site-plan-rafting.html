Overview

Purpose

We focus to provide you with the best guides and equipment to make sure you have the best ride! If your seeking adventure, safety is the first item you need to pack! Our company guides are trained and certified in swift water rescue, lifeguard trained and certified, wilderness First Aid trained and certified, CPR trained and certified, and BLS trained and certified, all guides are trained in wilderness survival techniques. Our guides go through an extensive and comprehensive training and certification process. Each guide must maintain strict health fitness to be a part of our team. We are not only taking you on a ride of a lifetime we are caring for your safety.

Audience

We can meet the adventure need for the individual, couple, family, and corporate family. If you are looking to seek adventure as a solo travel, a couple’s retreat, family bonding, school or corporate team building activity we are right for you. If you are 8 to 108 years old, we can create an adventure that is curated, and specific to your skill, risk taken, adrenaline seeking style. Our customers will find a price point suitable for every adventure budget friendly wallet. We provide group rate discounts, senior discounts, military discounts, and select weekday and holiday special pricing, school activity we have fundraising, and fieldtrip pricing available. We also provide a frequent Rapid Rider Card through our app RAPID ROARS where you can get points that can be applied to a free one-person trip, 25% discount on group rates or merchandise. If you like the great outdoors, hitting the rapids, seeking adventure, and expect to take a story or two home, you will find that we have the ride that will take you there on our rafts. We guarantee this experience will last you a lifetime of memories and bragging rights after conquering our rapids. We are easily accessible from mobile app at RAPID ROARS, our company website, or by QR code. The next rapid is yours!
